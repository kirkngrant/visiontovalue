The reality of technology programs

Transformation is failing at scale.

The numbers are not improving. Organizations invest millions — sometimes hundreds of millions — only to watch programs drift: status reports disconnect from reality, costs escalate without a credible forecast, decisions slow to a halt.

These are not people problems. They are structural breakdowns — gaps in SDLC compliance, misalignment between delivery and operations, and quality controls that exist on paper but not in practice.

The question is not whether to transform. It is how to be in the minority that succeeds.
